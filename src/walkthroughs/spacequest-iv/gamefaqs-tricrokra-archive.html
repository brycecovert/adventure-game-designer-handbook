Introduction 
=========================================================================== 
Another oldie from Sierra On-Line. 
It was also the first installment of Space Quest to use Sierra's icon click 
interface. 
The game should run fine on DosBOX. I had problems with SQ4 in ScummVM 
(crashing on vital locations of the game, rendering the game impossible to 
beat). Perhaps my copy got damaged over the years (floppies were never such 
a reliable storage medium anyway) but as a result I cannot (yet) recommend 
ScummVM is a good way to play this game. 
Let's just make a quick intro on stuff. 
First, abandonware is no legal concept, so don't think a download from it 
is legal. If you don't care about stuff like that, not my problem, but at 
least you now know the way it is by law. If you know any place where the 
game can be obtained LEGALLY, let me know I'd love to put it in my FAQ if I 
can verify this to be a legal place to download/buy the game from. 
Second, I'm not going to explain all icons. Mostly you can figure out 
yourself which icon to use to perform a certain action. If stuff is really 
odd on this, I'll name which icon you need, but otherwise, you can think of 
it, don't ya? 
Third, I'll write my FAQs this style 
*:> When I start like this you are in a new location. Sometimes I'll give a 
    brief description, so that you can verify you are in the correct spot. 
    = And this way 
    = I'll summarize 
    = all actions 
    = you need to 
    = take on.

Characters 
=========================================================================== 
*:> Roger Wilco 
     Our hero. A space janitor who always gets himself in trouble. Well 
    that's where you come in. You can get him out, right? 
*:> Rebel 
     This identity will be revealed halfaway the game, so I'll refer to him 
    as "rebel" during this FAQ in order not to spoil the game. 
*:> Sludge Vohaul 
     Yes.... he's back. How? Well, he's more dangerous than ever, that's 
    for sure, and his goons are hunting for you. 
*:> Professor Lloyd 
     A scientist who can tell you what is happening in Space Quest XII by 
    means of a holographic message. 
*:> Zondra 
     A girl you obviously meet in your future and which you were going to 
    dump and now she's mad at you about it. Little does she now that it's 
    your future self she's talking about. 
*:> Bikers 
     These bikers have something against you, and if they can they will 
    kill you. 
Well, let's get this show on the road. :)

Space Quest XII - Vohaul's Revenge II 
=========================================================================== 
Well, you are in the future somewhere on the planet of Xenon. The town is a 
mess, and everything here is dangerous.  
Best is to save right away as death is around every corner. 
Very important is the "zombie" who wanders town. You never know where he 
can pop up, but if he touches you he'll summon a killer droid that kills 
you immediately (once the touch is done, you cannot prevent that anymore). 
You'll have to forgive me I cannot cover that "zombie" in the actions you 
must take, as he paces around randomly. If you see him, just take a walk 
through the city and come back and hopefully it's gone then. 
*:> Ruined City - S 
    = Exit East 
   
*:> Ruined City - SE 
    = Pick up the rope 
    = Exit North  
   
*:> Ruined City - E 
    = Stand behind a pillar 
    = Use the rope on the floor from here 
    = Wait until the rabbit comes 
    = When it stands in your "trap" click with the hand icon on it to catch 
      it. 
    = Open your inventory and look at the rabbit to turn it around 
    = Take the battery from it 
     **** OPTIONAL *** 
    = Exit West x2 
   
*:> Ruined City - W   
    = Examine the tank 
    = Get the cylinder from it 
    = Put it back 
     I don't know what good this was for, but it scores points. 
    = Exit East x2 
   
*:> Ruined City - E 
     *** END OPTIONAL PART *** 
    = Open the grating and you'll automatically go to the area below 
   
*:> Secret room 
    = Get the jar from the table 
    = Examine the mat (and you'll press the button below in order to get a 
      secret message, which is pretty important to understand the situation 
      you are in now). 
    = Open the great cylinder to the west and enter it 
   
*:> Sewers - NE 
    = Exit W x2 
   
*:> Sewers - NW 
     This works very very frustrating, SAVE BEFORE YOU DO ANYTHING as this 
    can (and most likely will) go wrong over 13218941 times, as the way 
    Sierra scripted this is just plain HORRIBILE. 
    = Go south and some green slime will follow you (if it touches you are 
      are dead) 
    = Exit South 
   
*:> Sewers - W 
    = Stand in a sideway as close to the NS-corridor as you can and when 
      the slime passes you fill your jar with it. 
    = Now wait until the slime has disappeared 
    = Hit the ladder 
   
You will now see a ship land. After the animation you'll be in the ruined 
town West section 
*:> Ruined Town - W 
    = Exit East 
   
*:> Ruined Town - C 
    = Enter the ship and you'll be taken to Vohaul's base. 
   
   
*:> Vohaul's Base - Shuttle Bay 
    = Exit West 
   
*:> Vohail's Base - Time Pod Bay 
    = Just click on the open time pod with the hand icon and the guards 
      will ignore you. 
   
*:> Time Pod 
    = In some older versions you may now get the copy protection. I'd love 
      to put it in this FAQ, however, I cannot yet. Word has reached me 
      that Sierra has released the copy-protection, however before I put 
      this in the FAQ I first need to verify this. So you need your own 
      documentation (which you should have if your copy is legal). 
    = NOW TAKE A NOTEBOOK AND WRITE DOWN THE CODE YOU SEE ON THE DISPLAY, 
      IF YOU NEGLECT DOING THIS YOU CANNOT FINISH THE GAME ANY MORE!!! 
    = Now (as a quick way to get away from Vohaul's guards, I often press 
      the first keys from the top row from left to right and the first from 
      the left on the middle row. This will get you in Space Quest III. 
      Don't leave the pod, or you'll die. 
    = Just try any kind of combination at random now until you end up in 
      Space Quest X 
    = Press the red button on the left, to leave the time pod.

Space Quest X - Latex Babes of Estros (Strange planet) 
=========================================================================== 
You are on a strange planet. 
The items you should have on this moment are: 
    = Battery 
    = Buckazoids 
    = Slime 
None of these items are needed for the time being though, and by the time 
you need them you can still get them, though you can save yourself a lot of 
trouble if you got them now. 
Well, let's just push the red button on the left and get a view on where we 
are. 
*:> Exit West 
*:> Exit South 
*:> = You should see the silhouette of a young girl sneak away. The game 
      will note you have the feeling you are not alone here. 
    = Exit North 
*:> = Stand in the shadow of the rocks and you'll see the shado of a beast 
      flying over. 
    = Exit East 
*:> Exit South 
Now you'll be snatched by the beast and thrown into the huge nest 
*:> Nest 
    = Wait until a soldier of Vohaul's is being dropped in the nest (don't 
      worry, he's dead) 
    = Search him to find a rag of paper 
    = Unfold this rag and you'll see the part of a time-pod code you need 
      later. 
    = Leave the nest 
   
Now you'll be taken by Zondra and her girls. She's not pleased to see you 
again (remember you are in the future, meeting these girls is something 
that will happen in the future). 
Just watch the entire sequence. 
*:> Zondra 
    = Jump in the sub as instructed 
*:> Nasty situation 
     In the end you'll be facing a huge moster. 
    = Wait until the metal bars keeping you on your spot open up 
    = Press the button which the girls used before to kill your pants 
   
*:> Zondra's Cave 
    = Get one of the cylinders 
    = Shoot the monster with it 
   
This will take you to the next section of the game.

Space Quest X - Latex Babes of Estros (The Great Mall) 
=========================================================================== 
You'll be taken to Galaxy Galleria, the great shopping mall, and Zondra and 
the other girls are only interested in one thing.... SALE.... Well, they 
leave you alone now and you won't see them again. (Good riddance). 
Now one warning. DON'T TRY TO LEAVE THE MALL. One of Vohaul's soldiers will 
pop up and kill you if you try. So you are basically a prisoner of this 
mall until you finished this part up. 
Now the true adventure challenge of SQ4 finally begins ;) 
*:> Entrance 
    = Zondra dropped her ATM Card. Take it (yeah, we're gonna rob her) :P 
    = Exit East 
   
*:> Outside Software shop 
    = Try Zondra's ATM card on the money machine, however, as your don't 
      look like a cute blonde girl the system will reject you (this has to 
      happen before you can get into the actual puzzle to get this money 
      anyway). 
    = Step on the conveyor and stay on it until you reached "And Tall". A 
      gentleman's clothing shop 
   
*:> Outside Gentle Man's clothing shop 
    = Enter 
   
*:> Gentleman's clothing shop 
    = Talk to the robot and it will get you a new pair of pants 
    = Pay the robot 
    = Leave and go to the software store (and enter, I won't say that all 
      the time) :) 
   
*:> Software shop 
    = Check out the box to the west 
    = Grab some (worthless games which are all parodies to existing titles) 
      games and put them aside until you find the SQ4 hint book. 
    = Look at the hint book and when in the menu choose "keep" 
    = Pay for the hint book 
    = Open the hint book 
   
*:> Hint Book   
    = Go to page 4 
    = Click on the dot at the third answer of the question "I'm in the 
      stupid time pod, hwere else can I go". 
    = You get the first three digits of the code to get in a new time 
      sector, the three on the rag are the last three, write the code down 
      as this is your ticket to get out of here (once you have a time pod 
      at your disposal, which will happen eventually). 
    = You may also want to go to page 7 
    = Reveal first question, third answer to find a series of numbers. 
      Write them down, you need them later. 
    = Now close the hint book and go to Monolith Burger 
   
   
*:> Monolith Burger 
    = Speak to the manager twice 
    = When he offers you a job, accept it 
    = It's up to you if you play or not of skip it. Whatever you do you 
      will always be fired and kicked out of restaurant (if you skip the 
      mini game you will now have 68 buckazoids). 
    = Go to the entrance room (but don't leave the mall!!!!) 
   
*:> Entrance 
    = Pick up the cigar (Once you leave the mall to get into the next 
      section you cannot get the cigar any more, and then you got a 
      problem). 
    = Go to the ladies clothing store 
   
*:> Ladies clothing store 
    = Speak to the robot (if "she" doesn't help you, sty to ATM card again, 
      and this time it SHOULD work, but I guess it's buggy). 
    = Now that you are dress like a woman, pay the robot 
    = Go to the software shop 
   
*:> Software shop 
     The shop is closed now, but that doesn't matter as we didn't need to 
    be inside anyway 
    = Use the ATM card on the machine and choose "Clean House" (there 
      Zondra! That will teach you to mess with me). :P This scores you 2001 
      buckazoids 
    = Go to the Radio Shock (due to a lawsuit, some newer versions of the 
      game have this company renamed to: Hz so Good. In this FAQ I'll stick 
      with the original name though). 
   
*:> Radio Shock 
     Click on te screen below the robot and click the next menu items 
    = Catalog 
    = Electronic Gadgets 
    = Contine - keep clicking that until you reach the item "PocketPal 
      Connector" 
    = If you see some plugs click the upper plug in the right collumn 
     (in the html version on http://faqs.tbbs.nl I included a picture of 
    the plug you need) 
    = Top Menu 
    = Exit 
     Now go back to the lady's clothes store 
*:> Ladies clothing store 
    = Just go into the back room to get back into your normal clothing 
   
   
   
   
!!HALT!! 
Before you continue make sure you have the cigar butt the manager of 
Monolith Burger threw at you when he fired you. Beyond this point you 
cannot get it any more, and then you are in trouble. 
When you have at least that, go to the arcade hall and enter it. 
*:> Arcade Hall 
    = Go to the east part of the hall and a time pod with two of Vohaul's 
      soldiers will arrive. (Now this goes pretty well timed, if you take 
      too long, you'll get killed). 
    = Leave and go into the skate-o-rama. You can enter it through the 
      passages either at the ladies clothing shop or Radio Shock. 
   
*:> Skate-O-Rama 
    = Go into the anti-gravity field (and you'll swim) 
    = Swim up and exit up (Keep moving or you're dead) 
*:> = At the roof, try to swim to the second window from the left (if you 
      are in the west section, where I recommend you to be) 
    = Vohaul's goons will pop up and shoot you, if you keep moving, they'll 
      miss and loose control over their swimming 
    = Quickly go down and go to the Arcade Hall 
   
*:> Arcade Hall 
    = Hit the time pod 
    = Enter the code you got from the hint book and the paper rag and 
      you'll end up in Space Quest I

Space Quest I - The Sarien Encounter 
=========================================================================== 
Well, you'll be in Ulence Flats. The game tries to mimic the place very 
well as it looks back in the ORIGINAL SQ1 game (not the VGA remake, though 
this remake has the easter egg of the pod showing up) ;) 
Well, let's leave the pod, and get into action. 
*:> Ulence Flats - Time Pod 
    = Exit South 
   
   
*:> Ulence Flats - Droids B Us 
    = South 
   
*:> Entrance Bar 
    = Enter  
   
*:> Bar 
    = Go to the bar and you'll be kicked out by the alien biker gang. 
   
*:> Entrance Bar 
    = Have your revenge and kick the bikes. 
     Now you have to be careful as from this moment on the bikers will try 
    to kill you whenever they can. They will try to do so by driving over 
    you with their bikes. If a biker is coming you can hear the special 
    "biker" tune. Then wait until the right moment and use the "walk" icon 
    to avoid the attack. I cannot cover these attacks in this FAQ as this 
    can happen pretty random, and if you do not follow this FAQ straightly, 
    it may get different also. 
     Now a silly mistake, the bikes oddly change color (at least in the EGA 
    version) when the bikers mount them. :) 
*:> Tiny's used spaceships 
    = Exit East 
   
*:> Entrance Bar 
    = Enter the bar 
   
*:> Bar 
    = Pick up the book of matches lying on the bar 
    = Exit the bar 
   
*:> Entrance Bar 
    = Exit North 
   
*:> Time Pod 
    = If you played exactly the way I did, then you will very likely be 
      attacked by a biker here. Like I said if that happens, jump aside at 
      exactly the right moment (use the walk icon for that). 
    = Enter the time pod 
    = Next stop Vohaul's base in Space Quest XII. I told you before the 
      write that code down before you left SQ12, if you did not, you cannot 
      finish the game any more.

=========================================================================== 
Space Quest XII - Vohaul's Revenge II 
=========================================================================== 
Well before we continue, items you must have at this point are: 
    = Jar with slime (from sewers SQ12) 
    = Cigar (from mall SQ10, I don't think you can get this at this point 
      any more) 
    = Matchbook (from bar, SQ1) 
*:> Time Pod 
    = Exit the time pod 
   
*:> Time Pod Bay 
     The guards are gone now. 
    = Exit East x2 
   
*:> Entrance Tube 
    = Use the acid on the lock 
    = Enter the tube 
   
*:> Laser Tube 
    = Don't just walk on. If you pass any of the rings you'll die. 
    = Use the match book and the cigar and the smoke will reveal the lasers 
    = Use terminal 
    = Now try to guess the right degress to set all lasers perfectly 
      vertical. (you must always type three digits, however you can use 
      leading zeros if you want to be lower than 100). 
    = If you set all lasers correctly (you'll score points if you do), you 
      can now safely exit North 
   
*:> Robot Maze 
    = Now wait awhile and a robot will appear from the west. Exit south and 
      come back, if the robot is leaving the area to the North, you did 
      this well, if not you'll always end up dead. So best is to save 
      before you try this. 
    = Exit West x2  (don't worry about robot warnings, they won't get to 
      you) 
   
*:> Strange room 
    = Examine the keypad 
    = Code: 69 65 84 76 69 (if you mess up you'll die, so type this right) 
    = Enter the console room that is now revealed 
   
*:> Computer screen 
    = Drag the robot to the toilet icon (this will remove the robots from 
      the game) 
    = Now (yeah, we must do this), drag your brain to the toilet icon. 
     Now a countdown will start, if the countdown reaches 0 you are dead. 
    So we must be quick now. Very quick (this countdown will be there for 
    the rest of the game, but don't worry, you are about to win now anyway) 
    :) 
*:> Strange Room 
    = Exit East x2 
   
*:> Robot Maze 
    = Exit North 
*:> West (duh!) 
*:> Exit south (both stairs will do) 
*:> Exit West 
*:> = Enter the elevator 
    = Exit North 
*:> Exit East 
*:> North 
*:> Final Room 
    = Vohaul will have placed himself in the body of the rebel. Fight him 
      until he will stand still once more. 
    = Go down the ladder to get the diskette 
    = Put the diskette in the disk-drive 
     Now select the next items from the menu 
    = Disk Download 
    = Beam Download 
    = Disk Upload (make sure the display does not point to Vohaul, or 
      you'll be dead). 
   
CONGRATULATIONS! YOU COMPLETED SPACE QUEST IV!

Deaths 
===========================================================================

      drag the "SQ4" icon to the toilet in the supercomputer. Quite logical 
      since you deleted the game you were playing 
DIE:> If the counter runs out after deleting your own brain, you (quite 
      logically) die. 
DIE:> If you upload Vohaul's soul into the rebel's body, you'll be dead
