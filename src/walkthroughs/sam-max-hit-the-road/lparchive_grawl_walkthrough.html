Introduction



Who are Sam & Max?

Sam is a calculative six-foot dog wearing a suit and a fedora, while Max is a short and aggressive "hyperkinetic rabbity thing". Both enjoy solving problems and cases as maniacally as possible, often with complete disregard for the law. Driving a seemingly indestructible black-and-white 1960 DeSoto Adventurer, the pair travel to many contemporary and historical locations to fight crime.

Why the entire series? Haven't some been done?

Yes, some have been done. I originally wanted to start with season 3, then found out season 2 never was LPed before. Season 1 has, but it wasn't archived on LPArchive and is forever lost (for those without archives purchase). If I was going to do season 1, 2 and 3, why not start with the very beginning? After all Sam & Max Hit the Road is my favourite adventure game of all time and it's pretty short as well.

So I made my decision, first I'll do the original DOS game everyone knows and loves and then I'll move on to season 1, 2 and finally 3. This means I'll be going through a grand total of 17 games. I hope you brought lunch.
