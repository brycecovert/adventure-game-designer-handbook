NOTES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Loom is unique among LucasArts adventure games for its musical interface
- Players control Bobbin Threadbare, an apprentice weaver from the Guild of Weavers
- Puzzles are solved by composing "drafts" (musical spells) on a distaff
- The game features no inventory system - pure puzzle solving through melodies
