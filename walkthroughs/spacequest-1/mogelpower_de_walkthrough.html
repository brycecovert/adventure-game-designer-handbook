Space Quest 1 - Roger Wilco in The Sarien Encounter (e)

Space Quest 1 Walkthrough

Please note, that this walkthrough is for the remade VGA version of Space Quest 1. With 
this walkthrough, you will win the game with all 201 points!

The Arcada

When you start the game, you will be on the Arcada. You have just found out that the 
ship is being boarded by unknown aliens. It may be a good idea to get off this ship as 
soon as possible. Click the Inventory icon and you should have 3 whole Buckazoids. 
Click the Walk icon and walk to the top left of the screen. You will enter the Data 
Archive room. Click the Hand icon on the green chair by the CRT console. Click on Exit. 
Click the Walk icon and attempt to leave the room. The lab scientist will enter the room 
and fall down. Click the Talk icon on him and remember which cartridge he tells you. 
You will have to get out your manual for this one, I am not about to break copy 
protection laws to get you through this game. Click the Hand icon on the green chair and 
again on the screen. Enter in the correct code for the cartridge the man told you about. 
Once the robot brings it to you, click the Hand icon and take it. Total Points: 7. Click the 
Walk icon and exit the room to left. If you hear footsteps at anytime, click the Walk icon 
and exit the screen, then simply return and be faster about walking. Click the Walk icon 
and walk to the top left of screen. Click the Hand icon on the dead body at your feet and 
get the Keycard. Total Points: 8. Click the Walk icon on the top right of the screen. 
Click the Hand icon on the blue elevator doors. Click the Walk icon on the Star 
Generator Lab door on the bottom middle of the screen. Click the Hand icon on the 
Widget on the base of the Star Generator platform. Total Points: 9. Click the Walk icon 
on the bottom right of the screen and then on the bottom right of the screen twice. Click 
the Hand icon on the yellow and black elevator doors. Click the Walk icon on the right of 
the screen twice. Click the Hand icon on the orange buttons just above you and again on 
the Open button. Total Points: 11. Click the Inventory icon, click on the Keycard, and 
click the Keycard on the silver elevator doors. Total Points: 13. Click the Hand icon on 
the closet door and again to take the Spacesuit. Total Points: 15. Click the Hand icon on 
the button to your left and get the Gadget. Total Points: 17. Click the Hand icon on the 
button by the red doors, on the escape pod, on the seatbelt, on the red panel in front of 
you, on power button (the third one), and finally on the throttle. Total Points: 32. Click 
the Hand icon on the red panel and again on the Autonav button (the first one). Total 
Points: 34.

The Planet Kerona

Click the Hand icon on the seatbelt and again on the Survival Kit just inside the pod 
door. Total Points: 36. Click the Hand icon on the Reflective Glass in front of the pod. 
Total Points: 39. Click the Inventory icon and click the Hand icon on the Survival Kit. 
Click the Walk icon on the bottom, then right, and then top of the screen. Click the Hand 
icon on the Sticky Plant and get yourself a sample. Total Points: 41. By now the Spider 
Droid will fall out of the sky and start to chase you. Hint: It might be a good time to save. 
Click the Walk icon on the right of the screen twice, then the top, on the Orat's cave, and 
finally on the big rock to your left. Total Points: 51. Click the Hand icon on the only part 
of Orat that remains. Total Points: 53. Click the Walk icon on the bottom left of the 
screen. If at anytime you need something to drink, click the Inventory icon, click on the 
Dehydrated Water, and then click the water on yourself. Click the Walk icon on the 
bottom of the screen and get on the skeleton. Don't worry about the rickety bridge, you'll 
only go over it once. Follow it around until you fall into the elevator shaft. Total Points: 
55. Click the Hand icon on the Stalagmite. Click the Walk icon on the left of the screen 
and close enough to the grate for the monster to get it's tentacles out. Click the Inventory 
icon, click on the Sticky Plant, and give it to the grate monster. Total Points: 60. Click 
the Walk icon on the bottom left of the screen. Click the Inventory icon, click on the 
Stalagmite, and click the Stalagmite on the steam geyser in front of the door. Total 
Points: 64. Click the Walk icon on the bottom left of the screen. Click the Inventory 
icon, click on the Reflective Glass, and click the glass on the electronic beams. Total 
Points: 69. Click the Walk icon on the top right of the screen. This can be a tricky part, 
so you might want to save as you go. There is a clear path of where the acid is not falling. 
Simply click the Walk icon on those parts until you pass it. Total Points: 72. Click the 
Walk icon on the right of the screen. Click the Inventory icon and click the Hand icon on 
the Gadget. Click the Walk icon on the right of the screen. When the face is done talking, 
click the Inventory icon, click on the Orat part, and give it to him. Click the Inventory 
icon, click on the cartridge from the Arcada, and click the cartridge on the computer next 
to the skimmer. Be sure you write down the self-destruction code. Total Points: 77. 
Click the Hand on the cartridge and on the skimmer. Play the arcade sequence if you like. 
Total Points: 112.

Ulence Flats

When the strange looking man asks to buy the skimmer for 25 Buckazoids, tell him No. 
He will come around the other way and offer 30 Buckazoids and a Jetpack, tell him Yes. 
He will also give you some useful coupons. Total Points: 117. Click the Walk icon on 
the right of the screen. You might want to save here. Click the Hand icon on the pile of 
ashes twice. You should get around 10 or more Buckazoids. If you feel you got ripped 
off, simply restore and try again. Click the Walk icon on the left of the screen and again 
on the Rocket Bar entrance. Click the Inventory icon, click on the blue coupon, and give 
the coupon to the bartender. Click the Talk icon on the bartender twice and order two 
more beers. When you overhear the conversation, be sure you write down the sector. 
Total Points: 122. By now the alien using the Slot Machine should be zapped and you 
have try it out. Click the Walk icon behind you to stand up. Click the Inventory icon, 
click on the Widget, and click the Widget on the Slot Machine. Total Points: 127. Click 
on the Buckazoids slot three times and let em roll. Continue until you blow up the 
machine (when you have around 300 Buckazoids). Click the Walk icon on the bottom 
left, then the top, then left, then bottom of the screen. When Tiny is done talking, click 
the Walk icon on the top of the screen and he will follow you. Click the Hand icon on the 
silver ship in the middle of the screen, Tiny will sell it for 214 Buckazoids. Click the 
Inventory icon, click on the Buckazoids, and give him the money. Total Points: 131. 
Click the Hand icon on your new ship. Click the Walk icon on the left of the screen twice 
and on the Droids B Us entrance. After the salesbug is done talking, click the Inventory 
icon, click on the brown coupon, and the coupon on the salesbug. Click the Inventory 
icon, click on the Buckazoids. Wait until the NAV-201 is on the green screen and give 
the salesbug the money. Total Points: 135. Click the Walk icon on the left of the screen. 
Click the Hand icon on your ship. Total Points: 160. Get your manual out again and 
enter the code for the sector they told you about in the bar. When your asked to leave, say 
No.

Sarien Ship

Click the Hand icon on the handle to your left. Click the Walk icon on the side of the 
door, make sure you're out of sight. When the robot comes out to clean up the mess, 
click the Walk icon on the door. Make sure you don't get in his way. Total Points: 161. 
Click the Hand icon on the trunk. Click the Inventory icon, click on the Xenon Army 
Knife, and click the knife on the air vent. Click the Walk icon on the upper shaft at the 
top of the screen. There is another air vent at the bottom right, click the Walk icon and 
get as close as you can to it. Click the Hand icon on the vent. Total Points: 164. Click 
the Hand icon on the laundry door and again on the helmet. Total Points: 169. Click the 
Hand icon on the ID card at your feet. Total Points: 171. Click the Walk icon on the 
bottom right of the screen. Click the Hand icon on the elevator doors on the left. If you 
forgot that self-destruct number, click the Walk icon on the bottom right of the screen. 
Click the Inventory icon, click on the ID card, click the card on the red robot, and click 
the Walk icon on the bottom left of the screen. Click the Walk icon on the bottom left of 
the screen. Click the Hand icon on the elevator doors. Click the Walk icon on the top 
right of the screen three times. Click the Inventory icon, click on the ID card, click the 
card on the robot. Wait until the robot is gone, click the Hand icon on the counter panel 
to your left and again on the Gas Grenade. Total Points: 172. Click the Hand icon on the 
counter panel again. The robot will return with your Laser Pistol. Total Points: 173. 
Click the Walk icon on the bottom left. Click the Inventory icon, click on the Gas 
Grenade, click the grenade on the red robot at the bottom of the screen. Total Points: 
178. Click the Walk icon on the top left of the screen twice. Click the Hand icon on the 
elevator door. Don't worry about losing your helmet, you'll just have to run fast from 
now on. Click the Hand icon on the elevator again. Click the Walk icon on the bottom 
right of the screen twice and be fast. Click the Hand icon on the red robot. Total Points: 
181. Click the Inventory icon, click on the remote control, click the remote on the Star 
Generator. Total Points: 184. Click the Hand icon on the box at the base of  the Star 
Generator, enter the self-destruct code, and press enter. Total Points: 194. Click the 
Walk icon on the bottom left of the screen. Click the Hand icon on the green elevator 
doors. Click the Inventory icon, click on the Laser Pistol, click the pistol on the guard in 
front of the other elevator. Total Points: 197. Click the Hand icon on the elevator doors 
to the right. Total Points: 198. Click the Hand icon on the escape pod. Total Points: 
201.
