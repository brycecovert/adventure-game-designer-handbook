Broken Sword - Shadow of the Templars: Reforged is a remastered revival of the 1996 classic point-and-click adventure game of the same name (without the Reforged, of course). With remastered visuals, enhanced audio, and a whole new UI system, as well as restored audio lines and scenes reworked to reflect the original concept art, Broken Sword doesn't get any better than this.

Broken Sword: Reforged Interview - How Bringing The Game To Fans Has Changed Since The ‘90s

At Gamescom, we spoke with Revolution Software's Charles Cecil about Broken Sword - Shadow of the Templars: Reforged.

By  Meg Pelliccio

If you're finding that you need a little helping hand to get through all the puzzles within Reforged, we've got everything you need right here in our complete walkthrough. It's worth noting that our walkthrough encompasses the essential actions needed to progress only, and that you'll be missing out on a lot of optional (and often hilarious) scenes and lines by only doing the bare minimum. We recommend exploring the game to its fullest to appreciate it.

What's The Difference Between Classic And Story Mode?

Classic Mode is for point-and-click veterans and those who wish to undertake the full challenge of the game without necessarily getting any tips or hints along the way. All the red herrings and optional content to steer you away from the correct path is there, but if you do want a hint, you can still request one manually.

Story Mode is better for beginners to point-and-click games, or those who want less of a challenge, offering automatic hints as you play and removing any obsolete interactions and clues so there's less to muddle through.

Once you begin your game, you can change these settings at any time and also choose to customise the different options to best suit your own needs.

Can I Play With The Original PlayStation Graphics?

Yes, you can! Go into the settings and then under 'Video' you can choose between Original and Reforged graphics. Even with the original graphics, the UI will still be the new Reforged system.

Is There Chapter Select?

You'll unlock chapter select after completing the game, so you can go back and experience scenes in different ways to mop up all those achievements/trophies, or simply relive your favourite moments.
