Introduction\n\n===============================================================================\n\nFull Throttle is an interesting and original adventure game from Lucasarts. The \ngame starts with Malcolm Corley, the owner of a motorcycle company named Corley \nMotors, driving in a car with his employee Ripburger. Rip has plans of his own \nfor taking over the business when Corley dies, but he knows well that the last \nthing that Corley will do is hand the business over to him. So Ripburger kills \nCorley, blames the crime on a local motorcycle gang named the Polecats and then \ndecides to introduce himself as the new leader of Corley Motors.\n\nAnd that\u0026apos;s where we come in: As Ben, the leader of the Polecats. A very simple \ncontrol interface is used that is very similar to the one seen in Monkey Island \n3. The mouse cursor is moved over an object until it is highlighted, then the \nleft mouse button is held to show actions that can be used to interact with the \nenvironments. There is the usual look and talk actions seen in most adventures, \nbut there is also the taste and kick commands. While most games would have you \nfinding a key to open a door, Full Throttle just lets you kick it down.\n\nAnother difference from this game as opposed to every other LucasArts adventure \nis that most of the action consists of arcade sequences. One example of this is \nin a part of the game on the Old Mine Road, where the player controls movement \nwith the arrow buttons and finds new weapons by knocking the riders off their \nbikes. It fits the style and feel of the game extremely well, and the numerous \narcade sequences combined with the simple yet interesting puzzles make this yet \nanother excellent adventure game from LucasArts.\n\n===============================================================================\n\n2.                                Walkthrough\n\n===============================================================================\n\nAfter the introduction, Ben is found in a bin outside of the Kick Stand. Select \nthe hatch of the bin and Ben will punch his way out, then walk right and kick \nthe door of the Kick Stand. Enter the bar and grab the bartender, who gives Ben \nthe keys to his bike. Exit the Kick Stand and use the bike. We next find Ben on \nthe road, where another biker approaches him. Move with the mouse and use the \nleft mouse button to punch the other biker off his bike. After the cutscene \nwhere the wheel falls off Ben\u0026apos;s bike, he will be rescued by a reporter who \ntakes him to the town of Melonweed.\n\nSay \u0026quot;Well, I\u0026apos;ll let you get back to work...\u0026quot; to stop talking to Maureen, then \nget the gas can and hose on the desk at the right side of the room. Exit the \ngarage to talk to the reporter who saved Ben. Say \u0026quot;Well, I\u0026apos;d better take off.\u0026quot; \nto stop talking. Exit left to the map screen and walk to the trailer near the \ntop-left corner of the map. Knock on the door and wait for Todd to answer it, \nthen kick the door to launch him across the room. Open the cabinet above Todd \nto get the lockpick, and open the fridge to get the meat.\n\nWalk on the platform at the top-right corner of the room and get the welding \ntorch on the desk to return to Maureen\u0026apos;s garage. Walk left to return to the \nmap, and select the tower at the top-right corner. Use the lockpick on the lock \non the door, then get the lock from the floor and walk through the door to the \ntower area. Touch the ladder to set off an alarm, then quickly hide behind the \npillar at the left side of the area as the police hover sleds will soon come in \nto see what\u0026apos;s happening. As they see nobody in the area, they will guess that \nBen is hiding up in the tower and will climb up to check.\n\nOpen the gas cap on their hover sled and use the gas can on the gas cap. Use \nthe hose on the gas cap, then drink from the hose to get the fuel. The hover \nsleds will fly off to get reinforcements, while Ben will return to Maureen\u0026apos;s \ngarage to give her the gas can. Now all we need to get are the forks. Walk left \nto return to the map and select the junk yard at the top-left corner. Put the \nlock on the latch at the bottom of the entrance door and climb up the chain to \nenter the junk yard. Jump off the path to land down in the main area, and walk \nright to arrive in an area with lots of cars.\n\nPut the meat in the blue car below the press. Walk left to return the main area \nof the car park, climb up to the path at the bottom of the screen and walk \nright along the path to climb up to the control tower. Move the left lever down \nuntil the press is just above the car, then press the red button to lift the \ncar up. Move the left lever up until the car raises as high as possible. Exit \nthe control tower, walk left along the path and drop back down to the main area \nof the junk yard. Get the forks from the parts pile in the lit area of the junk \nyard, and Ben will travel back to the garage to give the forks to Maureen.\n\nIn the cutscene, Ben will discover that the road out of Melonweed is blocked by \nthe reinforcements that were sent for by the two hover sled police from the \ntower. Return to the tower at the top-right corner of the map and touch the \nladder again to set off the alarm. In the cutscene, Ben will automatically \nleave Melonweed on his bike to catch up with the Polecats. Ben will return to \nMaureen\u0026apos;s garage to find that part of it has been wrecked. Search the debris to \nfind the picture of Uncle Pete\u0026apos;s Mink Ranch.\n\nWalk left to return to the map, use the bike and exit Melonweed by the road at \nthe right side of the junk yard to return to the Kick Stand. Walk around to the \nside of the Kickstand and talk to Miranda in the dumpster, who will give Ben \nthe fake ID. Walk right and enter the Kick Stand to discover that the Polecats \nhave been accused for the murder of Malcolm Corley, and a roadblock has been \nset up near the Kickstand in an attempt to catch Ben. Give the fake ID to Emmet \nto travel to Uncle Pete\u0026apos;s Mink Ranch.\n\nWalk through the doorway to enter the house and move the pillow on the bed to \nget the tire iron. Use the tire iron on the chest to get the hose from inside. \nAfter the cutscene, Ben will arrive on a section of the road with the \nfertilizer spilled by the semitrailer. Use the tire iron on the wheel and then \npush the semitrailer over. Get some of the fertlizer, and then get on the bike. \nRide all the way up the road to the mink ranch at the end. After Ben is chased \nby Hector\u0026apos;s men, ride through the fertilizer in the road and they will crash.\n\nBen will now be in the area with the broken bridge, which we will have to jump \nlater on after collecting some important items. Drive left down the road and \nstop at the area where the blue hovercraft crashed. Use the tire iron on the \nhovercraft to get the hover craft unit. Get on the bike and ride up the road, \nthen left click when the mine cart entrance appears on the right to ride down \nto the mine road. This area consists of a curvey road that a variety of riders \nwith different weapons ride down.\n\nUse the mouse to steer on the road until Ben encounters one of the riders. The \nfirst rider will actually be the original Polecat leader Father Torque, so just \nsay \u0026quot;Well, take it easy, Father.\u0026quot; to continue on down the road. When Ben finds \nthe first fighter, keep moving right and tapping the left mouse button to \npunch. Continue punching until the rider falls off their bikes and Ben receives \na weapon. Some riders can only be defeated with certain weapons, while others \nare easily defeated with any of the available weapons.\n\nFirst of all try and find the Vulture with the chainsaw. Defeat her by throwing \nthe fertiliser to get the chainsaw, which can be used to defeat every rider \nexcept for the Cavefish and the Vulture with the booster. Next use the chainsaw \nto defeat the rider who has the chain. This is the only weapon that can be used \non the Vulture, so defeat him to get the booster from his bike. Use the \nchainsaw once again to defeat the rider with the plank, then approach the \nCavefish. Slowly move toward the cave fish with the right arrow key, but don\u0026apos;t \nget too close or Ben will slip in the oil.\n\nWhen the Cavefish raises his head, quickly move right and hit once with the \nplank to knock him off his bike for the goggles. Right click to wear them, and \nwait for the \u0026apos;Cave\u0026apos; message to appear. When it does, quickly left click to \nenter the secret Cavefish hideout. Continue down the two sections of road to \narrive in an area with the ramp. Push the ramp toward the bike, then use the \nramp to hook it onto the back of the bike. Get on the bike and drive right to \nthe area of the road with the fence.\n\nGet off the bike and unhook the ramp to push the markers off the road that the \nCavefish use to navigate. After the cutscene, Ben will return to the gorge. We \nnow have the booster, the hover lift unit and the ramp, so get on the bike and \nride toward the gorge to jump over it. Ben will then ride to Corley\u0026apos;s factory.\nWalk down the slope and right to the stadium. Look at the souvenirs in the \ngifts stall and then quickly get the bunny on the cord when Horrace looks away. \nExit the stadium and get on the bike at the top of the slope, then drive north \ndown the path to the field outside of the Vultures\u0026apos; hideout.\n\nPut the bunny on the field and it will run over one of the mines, causing the \nbattery that was in it to land near Ben. Get the battery and return to the \nstadium. Put the battery in the RC car and drive it north toward the stadium, \nthen ride it through the gate. Horace will now walk around to the other side of \nthe stadium to retrieve the car, so get the bunny box from the gifts stall and \nreturn to the minefield. Drop the bunny box on the field and get at least three \nof the bunnies before they explode.\n\nReturn to the stadium and get the second bunny box, then get back on the bike \nand return to the minefield. Drop the bunny box on the minefield and wait for \nall of the bunnies to explode. When all of the bunnies are gone, walk up to the \ntop of the path they created and put one of the bunnies from the first bunny \nbox on the field. Walk a bit higher up the path and put the second bunny down, \nthen walk up the path again and drop the third bunny.\n\nContinue up the path to enter the hideout where Maureen will accuse Ben of \nkilling her father and will tie him up. Say \u0026quot;Let me go, or else...\u0026quot;, say \u0026quot;I\u0026apos;ll \ncall you names!\u0026quot;, then say \u0026quot;Diapered Dyanmo!\u0026quot; to explain that Ripburger was \nresponsible for the murder. After the cutscene where the Vultures form a plan, \nBen will be in a car at the stadium. Drive up the ramp at the left side of the \nstadium to land on the brown car, which will stall. Hit the brown car over to \nthe bottom-right corner of the stadium, then keep moving it up to the top of \nthe ramp so that it lands on the floor below.\n\nBen will then hit into Maureen\u0026apos;s car, causing it to burst into flames at the \ncorner of the stadium. Ben has caught on fire in the special protective suit he \nis wearing. Run along the side of the stadium and it will catch on fire. \nRipburger\u0026apos;s men are still after Ben however, and he runs up to the top of the \nbrown car. Walk off the top of the brown car until the blue car starts to drive \nquickly toward Ben. When the blue car starts to ram the brown car, quickly walk \non top of the blue car. Walk off the car when it gets near the bottom-right \ncorner of the stadium, and walk into the fire to escape from Ripburger\u0026apos;s men.\n\nAfter the cutscene, Ben will return with Maureen to the Vultures\u0026apos; hideout. Say \n\u0026quot;I\u0026apos;ll see what I can do.\u0026quot;, then exit the hideout to return to the road near \nCorley\u0026apos;s factory. Walk behind the left side of the factory to arrive in an area \nwith a cracked wall with some meters on the left. There is a secret tunnel in \nthis area, but to open it the wall must be kicked in the right place at the \nright time. Look down from the left post to see a small rock at the bottom of \nthe wall. Kick the rock at the right side of this when the meters have stopped \nmoving to open the secret tunnel.\n\nClimb down this tunnel to enter Corley\u0026apos;s office. Look at the floor safe and use \nthe arrows to enter the number \u0026apos;154492\u0026apos;, then press the button to receive the \ncard and the tape from the safe. Walk right to the hall and use the card on the \ncard reader to enter the film projection room. Raise the motor lever and use \nthe lamp lever twice to break the projector, which will stop Ripburger\u0026apos;s \nspeech. Walk through the middle door and use the tape on the reel machine to \nshow Ripburger\u0026apos;s crime to the crowd watching his speech.\n\nHe makes his escape while Maureen comes on to tell the crowd what really \nhappened, and after the cutscene we next find Ben being chased down the road by \nRipburger on his truck. Open the panel below the window, and get Ripburger\u0026apos;s \ncane as soon as he leans out to close the panel. Open the grill below the panel \nand use the cane on the fan to pass through to the back of the truck. Use the \ntire iron on the right fuel line, and the truck will ride back into the plane \nthat was riding behind. Climb up the ladder to the cockpit and look at the \nmiddle screen to view the menu for the control systems.\n\nSelect Post Take-Off, Gear, Raise Gear, and the plane will then stop right on \nthe edge of the gorge. Ripburger will fly out of the truck and land on one of \nthe machine guns down below, so we still have to find a way to lose him. Climb \ninto the front of the truck and sleect Main Menu, Defense Menu, Machine Guns, \nControl, System Off, and Ripburger will fall down into the gorge. Quickly climb \nback into the plane to find that it is just about to fall off the edge, and Ben \ncan\u0026apos;t leave just yet or it will tip over the edge. With only a few seconds left \nto spare, quickly get on the bike to complete the game.\n\n===============================================================================\n\n3.                                  Full Throttle

1.                                Introduction

===============================================================================

Full Throttle is an interesting and original adventure game from Lucasarts. The 
game starts with Malcolm Corley, the owner of a motorcycle company named Corley 
Motors, driving in a car with his employee Ripburger. Rip has plans of his own 
for taking over the business when Corley dies, but he knows well that the last 
thing that Corley will do is hand the business over to him. So Ripburger kills 
Corley, blames the crime on a local motorcycle gang named the Polecats and then 
decides to introduce himself as the new leader of Corley Motors.

And that&#x27;s where we come in: As Ben, the leader of the Polecats. A very simple 
control interface is used that is very similar to the one seen in Monkey Island 
3. The mouse cursor is moved over an object until it is highlighted, then the 
left mouse button is held to show actions that can be used to interact with the 
environments. There is the usual look and talk actions seen in most adventures, 
but there is also the taste and kick commands. While most games would have you 
finding a key to open a door, Full Throttle just lets you kick it down.

Another difference from this game as opposed to every other LucasArts adventure 
is that most of the action consists of arcade sequences. One example of this is 
in a part of the game on the Old Mine Road, where the player controls movement 
with the arrow buttons and finds new weapons by knocking the riders off their 
bikes. It fits the style and feel of the game extremely well, and the numerous 
arcade sequences combined with the simple yet interesting puzzles make this yet 
another excellent adventure game from LucasArts.

2.                                Walkthrough

===============================================================================

After the introduction, Ben is found in a bin outside of the Kick Stand. Select 
the hatch of the bin and Ben will punch his way out, then walk right and kick 
the door of the Kick Stand. Enter the bar and grab the bartender, who gives Ben 
the keys to his bike. Exit the Kick Stand and use the bike. We next find Ben on 
the road, where another biker approaches him. Move with the mouse and use the 
left mouse button to punch the other biker off his bike. After the cutscene 
where the wheel falls off Ben&#x27;s bike, he will be rescued by a reporter who 
takes him to the town of Melonweed.

Say &quot;Well, I&#x27;ll let you get back to work...&quot; to stop talking to Maureen, then 
get the gas can and hose on the desk at the right side of the room. Exit the 
garage to talk to the reporter who saved Ben. Say &quot;Well, I&#x27;d better take off.&quot; 
to stop talking. Exit left to the map screen and walk to the trailer near the 
top-left corner of the map. Knock on the door and wait for Todd to answer it, 
then kick the door to launch him across the room. Open the cabinet above Todd 
to get the lockpick, and open the fridge to get the meat.

Walk on the platform at the top-right corner of the room and get the welding 
torch on the desk to return to Maureen&#x27;s garage. Walk left to return to the 
map, and select the tower at the top-right corner. Use the lockpick on the lock 
on the door, then get the lock from the floor and walk through the door to the 
tower area. Touch the ladder to set off an alarm, then quickly hide behind the 
pillar at the left side of the area as the police hover sleds will soon come in 
to see what&#x27;s happening. As they see nobody in the area, they will guess that 
Ben is hiding up in the tower and will climb up to check.

Open the gas cap on their hover sled and use the gas can on the gas cap. Use 
the hose on the gas cap, then drink from the hose to get the fuel. The hover 
sleds will fly off to get reinforcements, while Ben will return to Maureen&#x27;s 
garage to give her the gas can. Now all we need to get are the forks. Walk left 
to return to the map and select the junk yard at the top-left corner. Put the 
lock on the latch at the bottom of the entrance door and climb up the chain to 
enter the junk yard. Jump off the path to land down in the main area, and walk 
right to arrive in an area with lots of cars.

Put the meat in the blue car below the press. Walk left to return the main area 
of the car park, climb up to the path at the bottom of the screen and walk 
right along the path to climb up to the control tower. Move the left lever down 
until the press is just above the car, then press the red button to lift the 
car up. Move the left lever up until the car raises as high as possible. Exit 
the control tower, walk left along the path and drop back down to the main area 
of the junk yard. Get the forks from the parts pile in the lit area of the junk 
yard, and Ben will travel back to the garage to give the forks to Maureen.

In the cutscene, Ben will discover that the road out of Melonweed is blocked by 
the reinforcements that were sent for by the two hover sled police from the 
tower. Return to the tower at the top-right corner of the map and touch the 
ladder again to set off the alarm. In the cutscene, Ben will automatically 
leave Melonweed on his bike to catch up with the Polecats. Ben will return to 
Maureen&#x27;s garage to find that part of it has been wrecked. Search the debris to 
find the picture of Uncle Pete&#x27;s Mink Ranch.

Walk left to return to the map, use the bike and exit Melonweed by the road at 
the right side of the junk yard to return to the Kick Stand. Walk around to the 
side of the Kickstand and talk to Miranda in the dumpster, who will give Ben 
the fake ID. Walk right and enter the Kick Stand to discover that the Polecats 
have been accused for the murder of Malcolm Corley, and a roadblock has been 
set up near the Kickstand in an attempt to catch Ben. Give the fake ID to Emmet 
to travel to Uncle Pete&#x27;s Mink Ranch.

Walk through the doorway to enter the house and move the pillow on the bed to 
get the tire iron. Use the tire iron on the chest to get the hose from inside. 
After the cutscene, Ben will arrive on a section of the road with the 
fertilizer spilled by the semitrailer. Use the tire iron on the wheel and then 
push the semitrailer over. Get some of the fertlizer, and then get on the bike. 
Ride all the way up the road to the mink ranch at the end. After Ben is chased 
by Hector&#x27;s men, ride through the fertilizer in the road and they will crash.

Ben will now be in the area with the broken bridge, which we will have to jump 
later on after collecting some important items. Drive left down the road and 
stop at the area where the blue hovercraft crashed. Use the tire iron on the 
hovercraft to get the hover craft unit. Get on the bike and ride up the road, 
then left click when the mine cart entrance appears on the right to ride down 
to the mine road. This area consists of a curvey road that a variety of riders 
with different weapons ride down.

Use the mouse to steer on the road until Ben encounters one of the riders. The 
first rider will actually be the original Polecat leader Father Torque, so just 
say &quot;Well, take it easy, Father.&quot; to continue on down the road. When Ben finds 
the first fighter, keep moving right and tapping the left mouse button to 
punch. Continue punching until the rider falls off their bikes and Ben receives 
a weapon. Some riders can only be defeated with certain weapons, while others 
are easily defeated with any of the available weapons.

First of all try and find the Vulture with the chainsaw. Defeat her by throwing 
the fertiliser to get the chainsaw, which can be used to defeat every rider 
except for the Cavefish and the Vulture with the booster. Next use the chainsaw 
to defeat the rider who has the chain. This is the only weapon that can be used 
on the Vulture, so defeat him to get the booster from his bike. Use the 
chainsaw once again to defeat the rider with the plank, then approach the 
Cavefish. Slowly move toward the cave fish with the right arrow key, but don&#x27;t 
get too close or Ben will slip in the oil.

When the Cavefish raises his head, quickly move right and hit once with the 
plank to knock him off his bike for the goggles. Right click to wear them, and 
wait for the &#x27;Cave&#x27; message to appear. When it does, quickly left click to 
enter the secret Cavefish hideout. Continue down the two sections of road to 
arrive in an area with the ramp. Push the ramp toward the bike, then use the 
ramp to hook it onto the back of the bike. Get on the bike and drive right to 
the area of the road with the fence.

Get off the bike and unhook the ramp to push the markers off the road that the 
Cavefish use to navigate. After the cutscene, Ben will return to the gorge. We 
now have the booster, the hover lift unit and the ramp, so get on the bike and 
ride toward the gorge to jump over it. Ben will then ride to Corley&#x27;s factory.
Walk down the slope and right to the stadium. Look at the souvenirs in the 
gifts stall and then quickly get the bunny on the cord when Horrace looks away. 
Exit the stadium and get on the bike at the top of the slope, then drive north 
down the path to the field outside of the Vultures&#x27; hideout.

Put the bunny on the field and it will run over one of the mines, causing the 
battery that was in it to land near Ben. Get the battery and return to the 
stadium. Put the battery in the RC car and drive it north toward the stadium, 
then ride it through the gate. Horace will now walk around to the other side of 
the stadium to retrieve the car, so get the bunny box from the gifts stall and 
return to the minefield. Drop the bunny box on the field and get at least three 
of the bunnies before they explode.

Return to the stadium and get the second bunny box, then get back on the bike 
and return to the minefield. Drop the bunny box on the minefield and wait for 
all of the bunnies to explode. When all of the bunnies are gone, walk up to the 
top of the path they created and put one of the bunnies from the first bunny 
box on the field. Walk a bit higher up the path and put the second bunny down, 
then walk up the path again and drop the third bunny.

Continue up the path to enter the hideout where Maureen will accuse Ben of 
killing her father and will tie him up. Say &quot;Let me go, or else...&quot;, say &quot;I&#x27;ll 
call you names!&quot;, then say &quot;Diapered Dyanmo!&quot; to explain that Ripburger was 
responsible for the murder. After the cutscene where the Vultures form a plan, 
Ben will be in a car at the stadium. Drive up the ramp at the left side of the 
stadium to land on the brown car, which will stall. Hit the brown car over to 
the bottom-right corner of the stadium, then keep moving it up to the top of 
the ramp so that it lands on the floor below.

Ben will then hit into Maureen&#x27;s car, causing it to burst into flames at the 
corner of the stadium. Ben has caught on fire in the special protective suit he 
is wearing. Run along the side of the stadium and it will catch on fire. 
Ripburger&#x27;s men are still after Ben however, and he runs up to the top of the 
brown car. Walk off the top of the brown car until the blue car starts to drive 
quickly toward Ben. When the blue car starts to ram the brown car, quickly walk 
on top of the blue car. Walk off the car when it gets near the bottom-right 
corner of the stadium, and walk into the fire to escape from Ripburger&#x27;s men.

After the cutscene, Ben will return with Maureen to the Vultures&#x27; hideout. Say 
&quot;I&#x27;ll see what I can do.&quot;, then exit the hideout to return to the road near 
Corley&#x27;s factory. Walk behind the left side of the factory to arrive in an area 
with a cracked wall with some meters on the left. There is a secret tunnel in 
this area, but to open it the wall must be kicked in the right place at the 
right time. Look down from the left post to see a small rock at the bottom of 
the wall. Kick the rock at the right side of this when the meters have stopped 
moving to open the secret tunnel.

Climb down this tunnel to enter Corley&#x27;s office. Look at the floor safe and use 
the arrows to enter the number &#x27;154492&#x27;, then press the button to receive the 
card and the tape from the safe. Walk right to the hall and use the card on the 
card reader to enter the film projection room. Raise the motor lever and use 
the lamp lever twice to break the projector, which will stop Ripburger&#x27;s 
speech. Walk through the middle door and use the tape on the reel machine to 
show Ripburger&#x27;s crime to the crowd watching his speech.

He makes his escape while Maureen comes on to tell the crowd what really 
happened, and after the cutscene we next find Ben being chased down the road by 
Ripburger on his truck. Open the panel below the window, and get Ripburger&#x27;s 
cane as soon as he leans out to close the panel. Open the grill below the panel 
and use the cane on the fan to pass through to the back of the truck. Use the 
tire iron on the right fuel line, and the truck will ride back into the plane 
that was riding behind. Climb up the ladder to the cockpit and look at the 
middle screen to view the menu for the control systems.

Select Post Take-Off, Gear, Raise Gear, and the plane will then stop right on 
the edge of the gorge. Ripburger will fly out of the truck and land on one of 
the machine guns down below, so we still have to find a way to lose him. Climb 
into the front of the truck and sleect Main Menu, Defense Menu, Machine Guns, 
Control, System Off, and Ripburger will fall down into the gorge. Quickly climb 
back into the plane to find that it is just about to fall off the edge, and Ben 
can&#x27;t leave just yet or it will tip over the edge. With only a few seconds left 
to spare, quickly get on the bike to complete the game.

3.                                 Item List

===============================================================================

Battery
  Found by using the bunny on the minefield outside the Vultures&#x27; hideout. It 
  is used with the RC car near the Gifts stall in the stadium.

Bunny
  Found outside the Gifts stall in the stadium when Horrace looks away. It is 
  used with the minefield outside the Vultures&#x27; hideout to find the battery.

Bunny Box
  Found on the counter of the Gifts stall in the stadium when Horrace chases 
  after his RC car. It is used on the minefield outside the Vultures&#x27; hideout 
  to create a path leading toward the hideout.

Cane
  Found by opening the panel on the Ripburger&#x27;s truck and getting the cane when 
  he reaches out. It is used with the fan in the grill on the truck.

Card
  Found in the floor safe in Corley&#x27;s office. It is used with the card reader 
  in the hall to enter the film projection room.

Chain
  This is one of the items found in the arcade sequence on the Old Mine Road. 
  It is used to defeat the Vulture with the booster.

Chain with Skull
  This is one of the items found in the arcade sequence on the Old Mine Road. 
  It is a more effective weapon than the chain.

Chainsaw
  This is one of the items found in the arcade sequence on the Old Mine Road. 
  It knocks other riders off their with only one hit.

Fake ID
  After leaving Melonweed, this is found by talking to Miranda in the dumpster 
  at the side of the Kickstand. It is given to Emmet in the Kickstand.
 
Fertiliser
  After getting the hose from the Mink Ranch, the fertiliser is spilled from 
  the truck that tips over in the cutscene. It is used to defeat the Vulture 
  with the chainsaw on the Old Mine Road.

Gas Can
  Found in Maureen&#x27;s garage at Melonweed. It is used on the gas cap of the 
  hover sled after Ben touches the ladder at the gas tower.

Goggles
  Found by defeating the Cavefish with the plank on the Old Mine Road. They are 
  used to find the entrance to the secret cave.

Hose
  Found in Maureen&#x27;s garage at Melonweed. It is used on the gas cap of the 
  hover sled after Ben touches the ladder at the gas tower.

Hover Lift Unit
  After Hector&#x27;s men crash their hovercraft by driving through the fertiliser 
  that is spilled from the truck, the unit is found by using the tire iron on 
  on the hover craft. It is needed to jump the gorge.

Lockpick
  Found in the cabinet above Todd in the trailer at Melonweed. It is used on 
  the lock outside the gas tower in Melonweed.

Keys
  The keys are found by grabbing the bartender in the Kick Stand. They are used 
  to start Ben&#x27;s bike.

Picture
  Found by searching the debris outside Maureen&#x27;s garage after the scene with 
  Ripburger and Corley. It is not used.

Plank
  This is one of the items found in the arcade sequence on the Old Mine Road. 
  It is used to defeat the Cavefish for the goggles.

Photos
  Maureen gives Ben the photos after he proves that Ripburger is responsible 
  for Corley&#x27;s murder. The photos can be used on the easel in the film room in 
  Corley&#x27;s factory. Alternatively, the tape can be used on the reel machine.

Tape
  Found in the floor safe in Corley&#x27;s office. The tape can be used on the reel 
  machine in the film room in Corley&#x27;s factory. Alternatively, the photos can 
  be used on the easel.

Tire Iron
  Found under the pillow in Uncle Pete&#x27;s Mink Ranch. It is used on the chest in 
  the Mink Ranch to get the hose for the bike. It is used on the wheel of the 
  semitrailer after it has spilled the fertiliser. It is used on the hovercraft 
  to get the hover lift unit. It can be used as a weapon in the arcade sequence 
  on the Old Mine Road. It is also used on the right fuel line at the back of 
  Ripburger&#x27;s truck to make the truck ride into the plane.

Welding Torch
  Found on the desk in the room below Todd&#x27;s trailer. It is given to Maureen.
