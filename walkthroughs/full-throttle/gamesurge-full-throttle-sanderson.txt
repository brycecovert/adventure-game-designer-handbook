  <H4>HEY! SPOILERS! &nbsp;The solution to Full Throttle by LucasArts follows:</H4>
<BR>
<BR>
Are you sure?<BR>
<BR>
<BR>
 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Full Throttle Walkthrough<BR>
 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; -------------------------<BR>
<BR>
Click on top of dumpster. &nbsp;Exit to the right. &nbsp;Kick in <BR>
door. &nbsp;Grab the guy behind the bar. &nbsp;He will give you <BR>
your keys back. &nbsp;Go outside and start your bike.<BR>
<BR>
Punch the guy off his bike. &nbsp;Your bike will eventually <BR>
fall apart. &nbsp;When you come to, you will be in a house. &nbsp;<BR>
Look at the photo on the wall. &nbsp;Get gas tank and hose. &nbsp;<BR>
Go outside. &nbsp;Talk to Miranda about the ambush. &nbsp;Tell <BR>
her where it is. &nbsp;Ask for a ride. &nbsp;She will say she <BR>
can't. &nbsp;Exit to the left. &nbsp;She will sneak off in her <BR>
car. &nbsp;Exit to the left again. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Go to Todd's trailer. &nbsp;Knock on door and wait for him <BR>
to answer. &nbsp;Kick in the door. &nbsp;He is knocked <BR>
unconscious. &nbsp;Open the refrigerator. &nbsp;Get the meat. &nbsp;<BR>
Open the cabinet. &nbsp;Get the lock pick. &nbsp;Walk to floor <BR>
panel on the right. &nbsp;Get the torch. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Go to the fuel tower and pick the lock. &nbsp;Pick the lock <BR>
up. &nbsp;Go inside the door. &nbsp;Touch the ladder then run <BR>
quickly behind the object in the far left rear. &nbsp;Go to <BR>
the hovercraft and remove the gas cap. &nbsp;Use the hose <BR>
with cap. &nbsp;Use gas can with hose. &nbsp;Use mouth with hose. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Go to the junkyard, use lock on door latch. &nbsp;Grab the <BR>
chain. &nbsp;Go to the parts pile and grab. &nbsp;The dog will <BR>
chase you. &nbsp;Jump back down. &nbsp;Go to the right past the <BR>
wreck. &nbsp;Keep going until you see the dog again. &nbsp;<BR>
Quickly throw the meat into the purple car. &nbsp;Exit left. &nbsp;<BR>
Go up to the crane. &nbsp;Use the controls to lift the <BR>
purple car up. &nbsp;Exit left, get the fork from the parts <BR>
pile. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Go back to the fuel tower and touch the ladder.<BR>
<BR>
Look at the debris. &nbsp;You will automatically pick up the <BR>
picture. &nbsp;Exit left. &nbsp;Get on bike and leave on road <BR>
going up. &nbsp;Go around to the back of the bar. &nbsp;Talk to <BR>
Miranda in dumpster. &nbsp;Exit right. &nbsp;Go in door. &nbsp;Show <BR>
guy with knife your false ID. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Go in door. &nbsp;Move pillow. &nbsp;Get tire iron. &nbsp;Use it on <BR>
lock.<BR>
<BR>
Use the tire iron on wheels. &nbsp;Kick trailer. &nbsp;It will <BR>
fall over. &nbsp;Get on bike and go back to the mink farm. &nbsp;<BR>
They will chase you and crash by the trailer.<BR>
<BR>
Get off the bike and look at the sign to the right. &nbsp;<BR>
Read all pictures and plaques. &nbsp;Get back on bike and <BR>
ride to the left. &nbsp;Go back to trailer (wait for STOP <BR>
sign to appear). &nbsp;Use the tire iron on fender. &nbsp;You <BR>
will get the fan automatically. &nbsp;Go on bike exit south. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Go to the Mill Road. &nbsp;You will meet Father Torque. &nbsp;Ask <BR>
him every question possible, or he will constantly come <BR>
back to bother you. &nbsp;You won't be able to beat the <BR>
cavefish guy without the board. &nbsp;You can't beat the <BR>
vulture guy without the chain. &nbsp;You can get this stuff <BR>
by beating up the people who have it. &nbsp;Keep going until <BR>
you beat up the vulture guy AND the cavefish guy. &nbsp;You <BR>
will get the special glasses and the rocket booster. &nbsp;<BR>
When you have done both, hit the right mouse button to <BR>
put on the visor. &nbsp;Press the left mouse button when the <BR>
display reads CAVE (not EXIT!!!). &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Once in the cave, drive right, then south west. &nbsp;Grab <BR>
ramp twice to attach to bike. &nbsp;Get on bike and ride <BR>
east. &nbsp;Get off bike and disconnect ramp.<BR>
<BR>
Get on bike and jump the ramp.<BR>
<BR>
Go to stadium. &nbsp;Go to gift stand. &nbsp;Look at T-shirts. &nbsp;<BR>
Grab the bunny when the guy's back is turned. &nbsp;Grab <BR>
joystick and play with the car until it stops. &nbsp;Go back <BR>
to bike and ride north. &nbsp;Use bunny on minefield. &nbsp;After <BR>
it blows up, pick up the battery. &nbsp;Exit south. &nbsp;Go back <BR>
to gift stand. &nbsp;Use battery with the car. &nbsp;Drive car <BR>
north east into turnstile. &nbsp;Grab box of bunnies. &nbsp;Go <BR>
back to minefield. &nbsp;Use box on minefield. &nbsp;Quickly pick <BR>
up all the bunnies except one. &nbsp;Wait for him to blow <BR>
up. &nbsp;Walk on path the bunny made and release another. &nbsp;<BR>
Keep doing this until you make it to the other side. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Tell Maureen that her father was murdered. &nbsp;Say, "Let <BR>
me go or else", "I'll call you names", "Diapered <BR>
Dynamo". <BR>
<BR>
The demolition derby is impossible to win. &nbsp;Press the <BR>
`V' key on your keyboard to win the derby. &nbsp;Run by box <BR>
seats to set them on fire. &nbsp;The two guys in the blue <BR>
car will chase you on top of the brown car. &nbsp;When the <BR>
blue car rams you, jump on their car. &nbsp;When they drive <BR>
near the fire, jump off and stand in the fire. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Look at parts and examine all of them. &nbsp;Go back to <BR>
factory. &nbsp;Maureen will give you the pictures <BR>
automatically. &nbsp;Go behind the factory. &nbsp;Kick the wall <BR>
stone to the right of the stone underneath the far left <BR>
post when the meters stop clicking. &nbsp;Enter the hatch. &nbsp;<BR>
Grab the floor safe by the desk. &nbsp;Enter 154492 as the <BR>
code. &nbsp;Exit right. &nbsp;Use the card on the card reader. &nbsp;<BR>
Move light lever twice and the motor lever once. &nbsp;The <BR>
film will burn. &nbsp;Go in second door. &nbsp;Put pictures on <BR>
easel. &nbsp;<BR>
<BR>
Open panel. &nbsp;Quickly grab Rip's cane. &nbsp;Open grill and <BR>
use tire iron on third fuel valve.<BR>
<BR>
Go up ladder. &nbsp;Press computer screen. &nbsp;Pick "Take off", <BR>
"Post take off", "Gear", "Raise Gear".<BR>
<BR>
Go inside truck. &nbsp;Use computer. &nbsp;Pick "Main Menu", <BR>
Defense", "Machine Guns", "Control", "System off". &nbsp;<BR>
Exit into plane. &nbsp;Move left. Grab Bike.<BR>
<BR>
That is it!!!!<BR>
